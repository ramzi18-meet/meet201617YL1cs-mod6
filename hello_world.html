What is MEET?

MEET connects, educates and empowers the next generation of young Israeli and Palestinian socially minded entrepreneurs to make a positive social and political impact in the Middle East.
MEET is a registered not-for-profit organization with innovation hubs in Jerusalem and Nazareth.
